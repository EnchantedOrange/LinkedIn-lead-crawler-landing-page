How it works?

LinkedIn Lead Crawler is an extension for
Chrome, designed to automatically invite new
LinkedIn connections.

It's very simple to use.
Set up your preferences in 3 small steps and we'll
take care of the rest!

#1

First of all, choose amount of invites you want to send and time interval between sending the invitations.

We recommend minimal time interval of 5 seconds to avoid banning on LinkedIn.

#2

Select the circle of your future connections (2nd, 3rd of both).

Then choose countries, where you want to find professionals.

#3

Now it's time to choose your search keyword.

For example, if you're looking for recruitment specialists, type in: 'recruiter, HR'

Now make sure you’re logged in to LinkedIn and press ‘START’.

Lead Crawler will open a new tab, please, don’t close it — it’s important.

That’s all. Simple, isn’t it?
